## Temporal semantics and reasoning

- **Allen (1983)** [allen1983intervals] — the interval algebra that underpins canonical TLINK reltypes.
- **Pustejovsky et al. (2003)** [pustejovsky2003timeml] — TimeML, the reference for TIMEX/EVENT/TLINK.
- **ISO 24617-1 (2012)** [iso24617-1-2012] — the ISO-TimeML standardization.
- **OWL-Time (2022)** [owl-time-2022] — a symbolic-web-compatible view.
- **Verhagen et al. (2007, 2010)**; **UzZaman et al. (2010, 2013)** — TempEval shared tasks [verhagen2007tempeval], [verhagen2010tempeval2], [uzzaman2010event], [uzzaman2013tempeval3].
- **TARSQI / TTK** [tarsqi-ttk] — practical temporal extraction.
